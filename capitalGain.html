General Information About Capital Gain

Women-owned businesses have been skyrocketing in the past few years. The statistics show that from 2014 to 2019, women-owned businesses have increased by 21% Let's go!

However, money is still a big factor when starting a business. On average, to start a business in 2020, it takes around $3,000 to start (price varies depending on location of office).On top of that, it is a good idea to have a 6-month fixed costs up-front, or at least until your business has a stable holding. That's a lot of money!

Fortunately, there are many types of ways to gain capital for your business: small business grants, federal grants, and competitions.

Small Business Administration

Small Business Administration offer 4 types of investments:
1. Small Business Investment Company (SBIC)
2. Small Business Innovation Research program (SBIR)
3. Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR) program
4. Women-Owned Small Businesses (WOSB) Federal Contracting program
