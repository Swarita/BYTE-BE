TAPPING NEW AVENUES
"Kickante’s goals last year were to be more self-sustainable and to lower our crowdfunding fee. We achieved them!” reports Candice Pascoal. Kickante’s crowdfunding service has now helped a total of 75,000 projects, up from 25,000 a year ago, raising over 55 million Brazilian Real (US$17 million) and forging a community of one million people. “Our impact lies in getting more funding for more campaigns.
