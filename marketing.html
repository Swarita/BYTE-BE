Want to start marketing but don't know where to start?

HERE ARE SOME IDEAS TO HELP YOU GET STARTED...

PERSONAL WEBSITE

Display your content in orderly manner, can include pages for about us products, and customer satisfaction

Make use of resources such as Wix.com with pre-made templates, for a hassle free website design experience

Grab user's attention by a good use of UI/UX designing skills


TIKTOK

Find your niche on the platform TikTok

Join the creatos fund to put your name out there and get paid for posting content

Showcase your products by making packaging videos and shipping out orders


INSTAGRAM

Target your audience based on specific posts

Can measure engagement levels when a business profile is created

Make frequent product posts, including all new drops

Reach out to individuals who may be interested in similar areas as what your personal brand offers


BUSINESS CARDS

It's important to start networking in any way possible

Physical and even digital business cards can come in handy

Include Company Name, Your Title/Role, Contact Information, address, and Social Media Pages


HOST GIVEAWAYS & SEASONAL SALES

Engage your audience with frequent giveaways

Reach out to others for collabs with bigger prizes

Bring the users back with seasonal sales, so they look forward to visiting your brand again
